Lasagna recipe

Description

A slow roasted beef short rib lasagna recipe, instead of the usual beef mince makes a massive difference, it's worth the wait!

Ingredients

For the ragu

  • 1kg of beef short ribs
  • 2 onions
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 2 bulbs of garlic
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 bottle of red wine
  • parmesan rind (optional)
  • olive oil
  • 2 tins of tomatoes
  • half a tube of tomato puree
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • 1 bunch of basil
  • 3 bay leaves

For the bechemel

  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 200g cheddar cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 750ml milk

For the pasta

  • 200g pasta flour
  • 200g egg yolks

Method

  1. Season the meat on all sides with salt, and brown on all sides in a large deep pan, remove and set aside
  2. Finely dice the onions, carrots, garlic and celery and add to the pan
  3. Do some cooking!
  4. Garnish with freshly grated parmesan, finely chopped chives and a sprig of basil
