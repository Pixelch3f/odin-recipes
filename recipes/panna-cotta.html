Fig leaf and cardamom Panna cotta

Description

An interesting flavour combo using a unique ingredient, fig leaf is similar in flavour to coconut

Ingredients

  • milk
  • cream
  • caster sugar
  • gelatine
  • fig leaves
  • cardamom pods
  • figs
  • honey

Method

  1. Toast the fig leaves in the oven at 120c for 15 mins until crisp but retaining their colour, set half of them aside for the powder
  2. Do some cooking!
  3. To serve, turn the panna cottas onto a plate, pipe or bottle the fig puree onto the plate, quater some fresh figs, and dust the panna cotta with fig leaf powder in a seive
